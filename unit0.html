Course Overview 课程概述

What is Business Environments? 什么是商业环境？

Introduction to the Business Environments: Unit 0 sets the foundation for the full course journey.

From the Unit 0 lecture: this course gives an overview of the dynamic, multifaceted contexts in which businesses operate, and explains why understanding these environments supports better strategy and operations.

Why Study Business Environments? 为什么学习商业环境？

  • Understand opportunities and threats in changing environments.
  • Adapt to legal and regulatory changes to avoid business problems.
  • Develop practical reasoning for business strategy and operations.

  • CLO1: Describe and discuss the diverse environments within which business operates.

  • CLO3: Identify and analyse ethical and sustainability issues that arise in the business environment.

  • CLO5: Collaborate effectively with individuals from diverse backgrounds with inclusiveness and integrity.

Foundational Legal Thinking in Business

Unit 0 and the course outline emphasise problem-based legal questions, argument building, and IPAC-based structure in legal analysis tasks.

  • I (Issue): What legal issue appears in the business facts?
  • P (Principle): Which legal rule or principle is relevant?
  • A (Application): How does the rule apply to this situation?
  • C (Conclusion): What is the most logical legal outcome?

60-Second Active Recall
