Introduction to the Business Environments 商业环境简介

Businesses operate in a dynamic environment 动态环境 that directly influences their operations and objectives. 企业在动态环境中运营，这直接影响其运营和目标。

Understanding the importance of understanding the multiple dimensions of the business environment for today's business students is crucial. 理解当今商科学生了解商业环境多维度的重要性至关重要。

The business environments 商业环境 encompass all external and internal factors that affect a company's operations. 商业环境包含影响公司运营的所有外部和内部因素。

Internal vs External Environment 内部环境与外部环境

The Internal Environment of Business 企业的内部环境

Business owners and managers have significant control 重大控制权 over the internal environment of business, which includes day-to-day decisions. 企业所有者和管理者对企业的内部环境拥有重大控制权，包括日常决策。

Examples of Control Over the Internal Environment: 对内部环境控制的例子：

  • Choice of supplies 供应商选择 - Selecting which suppliers to work with 选择与哪些供应商合作
  • Hiring decisions 招聘决策 - Choosing employees and building the team 选择员工和建立团队
  • Product selection 产品选择 - Deciding what products or services to offer 决定提供什么产品或服务
  • Sales locations 销售地点 - Determining where to sell products 确定在哪里销售产品
  • Company culture 公司文化 - Establishing organizational values and norms 建立组织价值观和规范
  • Internal policies 内部政策 - Creating rules and procedures 制定规则和程序
  • Management structure 管理结构 - Organizing the hierarchy and reporting lines 组织层级和汇报关系

The External Environment of Business 企业的外部环境

Key Characteristics 关键特征:

  • External environmental conditions generally change constantly 不断变化 and are beyond the control 超出控制 of management. 外部环境条件通常不断变化，超出管理层的控制。
  • The external business environment is composed of numerous outside organizations and forces. 外部商业环境由众多外部组织和力量组成。
  • To compete successfully, business owners and managers must continuously study 持续研究 the environment and adapt 适应 their businesses accordingly. 为了成功竞争，企业所有者和管理者必须持续研究环境并相应地调整其业务。

Major Sectors of the External Environment: 外部环境的主要部门：

  1. Economic 经济环境
  2. Political and Legal 政治和法律环境
  3. Technological 技术环境
  4. Sustainability 可持续发展环境

Each sector presents unique challenges and opportunities 独特的挑战和机遇 for businesses. 每个部门都为企业提供独特的挑战和机遇。

Real-World Impact Examples 真实影响案例

Impact of Natural Disasters on Businesses 自然灾害对企业的影响

Case Study: Black Summer Bushfires 案例研究：黑色夏季丛林大火

Natural disasters, such as the Black Summer bushfires 黑色夏季丛林大火, can have a significant impact on businesses. 自然灾害，如黑色夏季丛林大火，可能对企业产生重大影响。

These events can affect the environment, businesses, tourism, and people's livelihoods for extended periods. 这些事件可能在较长时间内影响环境、企业、旅游业和人们的生计。

Impact Statistics 影响统计:

  • The Black Summer bushfires burned an estimated 18.6 million hectares 1860万公顷
  • Destroyed over 5,900 buildings 5900多座建筑
  • Killed at least 34 people 至少34人
  • The economic cost is estimated to be over AUD $103 billion 1030亿澳元, making it Australia's costliest natural disaster to date 经济损失估计超过1030亿澳元，使其成为迄今为止澳大利亚损失最大的自然灾害

The long-term consequences 长期后果 of such disasters can be uncertain and recovery can be slow – recovery of communities and ecosystems. 此类灾难的长期后果可能不确定，恢复可能缓慢——社区和生态系统的恢复。

The COVID-19 Pandemic and Its Impact on Business COVID-19大流行及其对商业的影响

Key Impacts 关键影响:

Multiple waves of infections 多波感染 and the emergence of COVID-19 variants. 多波感染和COVID-19变种的出现。

The COVID-19 pandemic has caused significant disruptions 重大干扰 to global supply chains. COVID-19大流行对全球供应链造成了重大干扰。

Factors Contributing to Disruption: 造成干扰的因素：

  • Factory shutdowns 工厂关闭
  • Changes in consumer demand 消费者需求变化
  • Restrictions on trade and movement 贸易和流动限制

Businesses have had to adapt quickly 快速适应 to these changes. 企业不得不快速适应这些变化。

The pandemic has highlighted the importance of supply chain resilience 供应链韧性 and the need for businesses to have contingency plans 应急计划 in place. 这次大流行突显了供应链韧性的重要性以及企业制定应急计划的必要性。

Opportunities in Crisis 危机中的机遇:

Some businesses have also found opportunities 机遇 in the crisis, such as: 一些企业也在危机中发现了机遇，例如：

  • The chance to reassess their supply chain strategies 重新评估供应链战略的机会
  • Improve efficiency 提高效率
  • Build stronger relationships with suppliers 与供应商建立更牢固的关系

Key Components of the Business Environment 商业环境的关键组成部分

Overview 概述

Fluctuations in the level of economic activity create business cycles 商业周期 that affect businesses and individuals. 经济活动水平的波动创造了影响企业和个人的商业周期。

Key Characteristics 关键特征:

  • During periods of economic growth 经济增长, unemployment rates are low, and income levels rise. 在经济增长时期，失业率较低，收入水平上升。
  • Inflation 通货膨胀 and interest rates 利率 (monetary policies 货币政策) change according to economic activity. 通货膨胀和利率（货币政策）根据经济活动而变化。
  • Governments use fiscal policies 财政政策, such as taxes and government spending, to stimulate or curb the level of economic activity. 政府使用财政政策，如税收和政府支出，来刺激或抑制经济活动水平。
  • The forces of supply and demand 供求关系 determine how prices and quantities of goods and services behave in a free market. 供求力量决定了自由市场中商品和服务的价格和数量如何表现。

Note 注意: The economic environment is covered in the Economics course 经济学课程. 经济环境在经济学课程中涵盖。

Overview 概述

Technology 技术, the application of science and engineering skills and knowledge, can stimulate growth under any economic system. 技术，即科学和工程技能和知识的应用，可以在任何经济体系下刺激增长。

Impact on Business 对商业的影响:

  • New equipment and software that improve productivity 生产力 and reduce costs can be among a company's most valuable assets. 提高生产力和降低成本的新设备和软件可能是公司最有价值的资产之一。
  • Productivity 生产力 is defined as the amount of goods and services one worker can produce. 生产力被定义为一个工人可以生产的商品和服务数量。
  • A nation's ability to maintain and build wealth depends largely on the speed and effectiveness with which it uses technology. 一个国家维持和创造财富的能力在很大程度上取决于其使用技术的速度和有效性。

Applications of Technology 技术应用:

  • Invent and adapt more efficient equipment 发明和改进更高效的设备
  • Improve manufacturing productivity 提高制造生产力
  • Develop new products 开发新产品
  • Process information 处理信息

The argument is that in the long run, permanent growth 永久增长 is only achieved by technological progress 技术进步. 论点是，从长远来看，永久增长只能通过技术进步实现。

Many businesses use technology to create change, improve efficiencies, and streamline operations. 许多企业使用技术来创造变革、提高效率和精简运营。

Note 注意: The technological environment is not covered in this course. It is mentioned in the Sustainability Environment in this course. 本课程不涵盖技术环境。它在本课程的可持续发展环境中提及。

Overview 概述

The political environment provides businesses with an understanding of how political and legal systems 政治和法律体系 shape the business landscape. 政治环境使企业了解政治和法律体系如何塑造商业格局。

Key Aspects 关键方面:

  • The structure and function of government can impact business operations through laws and regulations 法律法规. 政府的结构和职能可以通过法律法规影响商业运营。
  • Political decisions 政治决策, policies, and governance can influence the business environment. 政治决策、政策和治理可以影响商业环境。
  • The amount of government activity, the types of laws it passes, and the general political stability 政治稳定性 of a government are key components of the political climate. 政府活动的数量、其通过的法律类型以及政府的总体政治稳定性是政治气候的关键组成部分。

Example: Tobacco Plain Packaging Act 案例：烟草平装法案

In Australia, the government passed laws to change to plain packaging of cigarettes 香烟平装. 在澳大利亚，政府通过了改为香烟平装的法律。

In Australia, the government enacted the Tobacco Plain Packaging Act 烟草平装法案 in 2011. This legislation made Australia the first country in the world to require tobacco products to be sold in plain packaging. 2011年，澳大利亚政府颁布了烟草平装法案。这项立法使澳大利亚成为世界上第一个要求烟草产品以平装形式销售的国家。

The law applies to anyone who: 该法律适用于以下人员：

  • Sells, offers to sell, supplies, packages, or manufactures tobacco products in Australia 在澳大利亚销售、提供销售、供应、包装或制造烟草产品

Under the plain packaging laws, all tobacco products must: 根据平装法律，所有烟草产品必须：

  • Be packaged in a certain colour 以特定颜色包装
  • Display brand names in certain ways 以特定方式显示品牌名称
  • Display the required text and graphic health warnings 显示所需的文字和图形健康警告
  • Not display logos, brand images, or promotional text 不显示标志、品牌形象或促销文字

This is an example of how government regulations 政府法规 can directly impact business operations and public health. 这是政府法规如何直接影响商业运营和公共卫生的一个例子。

It also underscores the importance for businesses to stay informed about changes in the legal and regulatory environment in which they operate. 它还强调了企业了解其运营环境中法律和监管环境变化的重要性。

Course Coverage 课程涵盖: The Australian government/political environment is covered in Unit 1 单元1 in this course. 本课程单元1涵盖澳大利亚政府/政治环境。

Overview 概述

The legal environment of business 商业法律环境 refers to the legal system, framework of laws, regulations, and legal principles that govern and impact business activities. 商业法律环境是指管理和影响商业活动的法律体系、法律框架、法规和法律原则。

Key Characteristics 关键特征:

  • The legal environment of business is composed of laws that come from various sources 各种来源. 商业法律环境由来自各种来源的法律组成。
  • This environment is influenced by the needs and demands of the business community, consumers, and government. 这个环境受到商业界、消费者和政府的需求和要求的影响。
  • Understanding regulations helps businesses adapt to legal challenges 适应法律挑战. 理解法规帮助企业适应法律挑战。

Importance for Businesses 对企业的重要性:

It's crucial for businesses to stay informed about changes in the legal and regulatory environment in which they operate. 企业了解其运营环境中法律和监管环境的变化至关重要。

Understanding the legal environment helps businesses: 理解法律环境帮助企业：

  • Avoid problems 避免问题
  • Maintain reputation 维护声誉
  • Make informed decisions 做出明智决策

Course Coverage 课程涵盖: The Australian legal environment for business is covered in Units 2 to 8 单元2至8 in this course. 本课程单元2至8涵盖澳大利亚商业法律环境。

Overview 概述

Sustainability 可持续发展 is the main overarching theme in assessing the broad dimensions of the business environment. 可持续发展是评估商业环境广泛维度的主要总体主题。

Key Principles 关键原则:

  • In today's world, threats can emerge quickly and spread rapidly, as exemplified by the COVID-19 pandemic. 在当今世界，威胁可以迅速出现并快速传播，COVID-19大流行就是例证。
  • Sustainability rests on the principle that we should not jeopardise the ability of future generations 危及后代的能力 to fulfill their needs through our current actions. 可持续发展基于这样的原则：我们不应通过当前的行动危及后代满足其需求的能力。
  • While often associated with environmental conservation, sustainability is much broader 更广泛, encompassing all aspects of the business environment, including human rights and societies. 虽然经常与环境保护联系在一起，但可持续发展范围更广，包括商业环境的所有方面，包括人权和社会。

Business Implications 商业影响:

  • The principle of sustainability encourages businesses to consider a broad range of stakeholders 广泛的利益相关者, both present and future. 可持续发展原则鼓励企业考虑现在和未来的广泛利益相关者。
  • Sustainability requires changes towards a better, cleaner, more ethical 更好、更清洁、更道德 way of doing things. 可持续发展需要向更好、更清洁、更道德的做事方式转变。
  • Governments and regulators 政府和监管机构 play crucial roles in implementing policies and laws that can make a lasting difference. 政府和监管机构在实施能够产生持久影响的政策和法律方面发挥着至关重要的作用。

Course Coverage 课程涵盖: The sustainability environment for business is covered in Units 9 to 11 单元9至11 in this course. 本课程单元9至11涵盖商业可持续发展环境。

Tutorial Questions and Activities 辅导问题和活动

Icebreaker Activity: Common Ground 破冰活动：共同点

Objective 目标:

The goal is to find interesting things you have in common with your partner, to help you get to know your tutor and each other better by discovering shared interests and experiences. 目标是找到你与伙伴的有趣共同点，通过发现共同兴趣和经历来帮助你更好地了解你的导师和彼此。

Instructions 说明:

  1. Pair up 配对: Pair up with a classmate you have not worked closely with before. 与以前没有密切合作过的同学配对。
  2. Find common ground 寻找共同点: Spend 5 minutes talking with your partner. Try to find as many things you have in common as possible. 花5分钟与你的伙伴交谈。尽可能找到你们的共同点。
  3. Share with the class 与全班分享: After the discussion, each pair will share one or two interesting commonalities with the class. 讨论后，每对将与全班分享一两个有趣的共同点。

Possible topics to explore 可能探索的话题:

  • Hobbies and activities 爱好和活动
  • Travel experiences 旅行经历
  • Books, movies, or music preferences 书籍、电影或音乐偏好
  • Academic or career goals 学术或职业目标
  • Unusual talents or skills 不寻常的才能或技能
