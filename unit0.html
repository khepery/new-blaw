Course Overview 课程概述

What is Business Environments? 什么是商业环境？

Business Environments (BE) is a comprehensive course that examines the legal, regulatory, economic, and social contexts in which businesses operate. This course provides essential knowledge for understanding how businesses interact with their environment and comply with legal obligations.

商业环境是一门综合性课程，研究企业运营的法律、监管、经济和社会环境。本课程提供了理解企业如何与环境互动并遵守法律义务的基本知识。

Course Structure 课程结构

12 Units spanning 13 weeks:

  • Unit 0: Introduction to Business Environments
  • Unit 1: Australian Legal Foundation
  • Unit 2: Australian Legal System
  • Unit 3: Business Structures
  • Unit 4: Contracts Part 1
  • Unit 5: Contracts Part 2
  • Unit 6: Negligence Part 1
  • Unit 7: Negligence Part 2 & Misstatement
  • Unit 8: Consumer Protection
  • Unit 9-11: Sustainability (Parts 1-3)

Why Study Business Environments? 为什么学习商业环境？

  • Legal Compliance: Understand legal obligations and avoid costly mistakes 理解法律义务并避免代价高昂的错误
  • Risk Management: Identify and mitigate business risks 识别和减轻商业风险
  • Strategic Decision-Making: Make informed decisions considering legal and ethical implications 考虑法律和道德影响做出明智决策
  • Sustainability: Integrate ESG principles into business practices 将ESG原则融入商业实践
  • Professional Development: Essential knowledge for business careers 商业职业生涯的基本知识

Assessment Structure 评估结构

Assessment Tasks 评估任务

1. Tutorial Participation (10%)

Format: Active participation in weekly tutorials 每周辅导课的积极参与

Purpose: Engage with course materials and develop critical thinking skills

  • Preparation: Complete pre-tutorial readings and activities
  • Engagement: Contribute to discussions and group activities
  • Professionalism: Attend regularly and participate constructively

2. Mid-Semester Assessment (40%)

Format: Written assignment or examination 书面作业或考试

Coverage: Units 0-5 (Legal foundations, business structures, contracts)

Skills Assessed:

  • Understanding of legal principles
  • Application of IPAC method
  • Analysis of case law
  • Written communication skills

3. Final Examination (50%)

Format: Comprehensive final exam 综合期末考试

Coverage: All units (0-11) with emphasis on Units 6-11

Question Types:

  • Multiple choice questions (knowledge and application)
  • Short answer questions (explanation and analysis)
  • Problem-solving scenarios (IPAC method)
  • Essay questions (critical analysis and evaluation)

Important Dates 重要日期

  • Week 1-6: Regular tutorial participation
  • Week 7: Mid-Semester Break
  • Around Week 8-9: Mid-Semester Assessment
  • Week 8-13: Continued tutorial participation
  • Examination Period: Final Examination

Note: Specific dates will be confirmed in the course outline and Moodle.

Course Learning Outcomes 课程学习成果

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

成功完成本课程后，学生将能够：

1. Legal Knowledge 法律知识

  • LO1: Demonstrate understanding of the Australian legal system and its application to business 展示对澳大利亚法律体系及其在商业中应用的理解
  • LO2: Identify and explain key legal principles in contracts, torts, and business structures 识别并解释合同、侵权和商业结构中的关键法律原则
  • LO3: Apply consumer protection laws and understand compliance obligations 应用消费者保护法并理解合规义务

2. Analytical Skills 分析技能

  • LO4: Analyze legal problems using structured problem-solving methods (IPAC) 使用结构化问题解决方法(IPAC)分析法律问题
  • LO5: Evaluate case law and apply precedents to business scenarios 评估判例法并将先例应用于商业场景
  • LO6: Critically assess business decisions from legal and ethical perspectives 从法律和道德角度批判性评估商业决策

3. Sustainability & Ethics 可持续发展与道德

  • LO7: Understand ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) frameworks and their business applications 理解ESG框架及其商业应用
  • LO8: Evaluate corporate social responsibility and sustainability practices 评估企业社会责任和可持续发展实践
  • LO9: Apply environmental law and climate change legislation to business contexts 将环境法和气候变化立法应用于商业环境

4. Professional Skills 专业技能

  • LO10: Communicate legal concepts clearly in both written and oral formats 以书面和口头形式清晰地传达法律概念
  • LO11: Research and reference legal materials using appropriate citation methods 使用适当的引用方法研究和引用法律材料
  • LO12: Work effectively in teams to solve legal business problems 有效团队合作解决法律商业问题
