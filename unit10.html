Unit 10: Sustainability Part 2

Unit 10: Sustainability Part 2 单元10：可持续发展（二）

Welcome to Unit 10 欢迎来到第十单元

This unit focuses on environmental law and regulation, examining how legal frameworks address environmental challenges in business.

本单元重点关注环境法律法规，研究法律框架如何应对商业中的环境挑战。

Topics Covered 涵盖主题

1. Environmental Law and Regulation 环境法律法规

Legal frameworks protecting the environment, including:

保护环境的法律框架，包括：

  • Environment Protection and Biodiversity Conservation Act 1999 (EPBC Act) 1999年环境保护和生物多样性保护法
  • National Environment Protection Measures 国家环境保护措施
  • State and territory environmental laws 州和领地环境法

2. Climate Change Legislation 气候变化立法

Laws addressing greenhouse gas emissions and climate adaptation:

解决温室气体排放和气候适应的法律：

  • National Greenhouse and Energy Reporting Act 国家温室气体和能源报告法
  • Renewable Energy Target 可再生能源目标
  • Paris Agreement commitments 巴黎协定承诺

3. Carbon Pricing Mechanisms 碳定价机制

Economic tools to reduce emissions:

减少排放的经济工具：

  • Carbon taxes 碳税
  • Emissions trading schemes 排放交易计划
  • Safeguard Mechanism 保障机制

4. Corporate Environmental Responsibility 企业环境责任

Businesses' obligations to minimize environmental impact through:

企业通过以下方式最小化环境影响的义务：

  • Environmental management systems 环境管理系统
  • Environmental reporting and disclosure 环境报告和披露
  • Due diligence in supply chains 供应链尽职调查

5. Environmental Impact Assessments 环境影响评估

Evaluating potential environmental effects of proposed projects before approval.

在批准之前评估拟议项目的潜在环境影响。

6. Pollution Control and Prevention 污染控制与预防

Legal requirements for managing air, water, soil, and noise pollution.

管理空气、水、土壤和噪音污染的法律要求。

7. Renewable Energy and Green Technology 可再生能源与绿色技术

Legal incentives and frameworks supporting clean energy transition.

支持清洁能源转型的法律激励和框架。

Key Learning Outcomes 主要学习成果

By the end of this unit, you should be able to:

在本单元结束时，您应该能够：

  • Understand key environmental legislation in Australia 了解澳大利亚的关键环境立法
  • Explain climate change mitigation strategies 解释气候变化缓解策略
  • Analyze corporate environmental responsibilities 分析企业环境责任
  • Apply environmental law to business scenarios 将环境法应用于商业场景
