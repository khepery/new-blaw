Unit 11: Sustainability Part 3 单元11：可持续发展（三）

Welcome to Unit 11 欢迎来到第十一单元

This final unit explores social sustainability, governance frameworks, and future trends in business environments.

本最后单元探讨社会可持续性、治理框架以及商业环境的未来趋势。

Topics Covered 涵盖主题

1. Social Sustainability 社会可持续性

Creating equitable and inclusive societies through business:

通过商业创造公平和包容的社会：

  • Fair labor practices 公平劳工实践
  • Workplace diversity and inclusion 工作场所多样性和包容性
  • Community engagement 社区参与
  • Social impact investing 社会影响力投资

2. Human Rights in Business 商业中的人权

UN Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights require companies to respect human rights throughout their operations and supply chains.

联合国工商企业与人权指导原则要求公司在其运营和供应链中尊重人权。

3. Governance Frameworks 治理框架

Structures ensuring ethical and transparent business conduct:

确保道德和透明商业行为的结构：

  • Corporate governance codes 公司治理准则
  • Board diversity and independence 董事会多样性和独立性
  • Risk management systems 风险管理系统
  • Anti-corruption measures 反腐败措施

4. Sustainable Business Practices 可持续商业实践

Implementing sustainability across business operations:

在业务运营中实施可持续发展：

  • Circular economy models 循环经济模式
  • Sustainable supply chain management 可持续供应链管理
  • Green procurement 绿色采购
  • Waste reduction strategies 减少废物策略

5. Corporate Reporting and Transparency 企业报告与透明度

Integrated reporting frameworks combining financial and non-financial information (sustainability, social impact).

整合财务和非财务信息（可持续性、社会影响）的综合报告框架。

6. Future Trends in Business Environments 商业环境的未来趋势

Emerging developments shaping sustainable business:

塑造可持续商业的新兴发展：

  • Digital transformation and sustainability 数字化转型与可持续性
  • Impact of AI and automation 人工智能和自动化的影响
  • Stakeholder capitalism 利益相关者资本主义
  • Net-zero commitments 净零排放承诺

7. Integrated Thinking and Reporting 综合思维与报告

Holistic approach connecting strategy, governance, performance, and prospects in the context of creating value.

在创造价值的背景下连接战略、治理、绩效和前景的整体方法。

Key Learning Outcomes 主要学习成果

By the end of this unit, you should be able to:

在本单元结束时，您应该能够：

  • Understand social sustainability in business contexts 理解商业环境中的社会可持续性
  • Apply governance frameworks to ensure ethical conduct 应用治理框架确保道德行为
  • Implement sustainable business practices 实施可持续商业实践
  • Anticipate future trends in business environments 预测商业环境的未来趋势

Course Conclusion 课程总结

Congratulations on reaching the final unit! You have now completed the Business Environments course journey from legal foundations to sustainability.

恭喜您到达最后一个单元！您现在已经完成了从法律基础到可持续发展的商业环境课程之旅。
