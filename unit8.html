Unit 8: Consumer Protection 单元8：消费者保护

Welcome to Unit 8 欢迎来到第八单元

This unit provides comprehensive coverage of the Australian Consumer Law (ACL) which protects consumers from unfair business practices and ensures fair trading. The ACL is Schedule 2 of the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 (Cth).

本单元全面涵盖澳大利亚消费者法(ACL)，该法保护消费者免受不公平商业行为的侵害并确保公平交易。ACL是2010年联邦竞争与消费者法附表2。

Topics Covered 涵盖主题

1. Australian Consumer Law (ACL) Overview 澳大利亚消费者法概述

What is the ACL? 什么是ACL？

The ACL is a national law that:

  • Applies uniformly across Australia (Commonwealth, States, and Territories)
  • Replaces previous state and territory consumer protection laws
  • Is Schedule 2 of the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 (Cth)
  • Enforced by ACCC and state/territory consumer protection agencies
Key Protections 主要保护
  • Consumer guarantees (automatic rights when buying goods/services)
  • Prohibition of misleading and deceptive conduct
  • Prohibition of unconscionable conduct
  • Protection from unfair contract terms
  • Product safety standards and recalls
  • Clear remedies for breaches
Who is Protected? 谁受保护？

"Consumer" under ACL means person who:

  1. Acquires goods/services for $100,000 or less, OR
  2. Acquires goods/services normally used for personal, domestic or household use (regardless of price), OR
  3. Acquires vehicle/trailer for use principally to transport goods on public roads

Note: Different sections have different definitions - check each provision

2. Misleading and Deceptive Conduct (s18 ACL) 误导和欺骗性行为

Section 18(1): The Key Prohibition 关键禁止

"A person must not, in trade or commerce, engage in conduct that is misleading or deceptive or is likely to mislead or deceive."

一个人不得在贸易或商业中从事误导或欺骗或可能误导或欺骗的行为。

Elements 要素
  1. Conduct: Act or omission (including silence in some circumstances) 行为或不作为
  2. In trade or commerce: Business or commercial context 在商业环境中
  3. Misleading/deceptive: Likely to lead into error or create false impression 可能导致错误或产生虚假印象

Test: Objective - what would reasonable person think? 客观标准-合理人会怎么想？

Examples of Misleading Conduct 误导行为的例子
  • False advertising: Claims products have qualities they don't possess
  • Bait advertising: Advertising products not reasonably available
  • Price representations: Fake "was/now" pricing, false discounts
  • Country of origin: Misrepresenting where products made
  • Quality/performance: Exaggerating product capabilities
  • Testimonials: Fake reviews or endorsements
  • Comparative advertising: False comparisons with competitors
Key Cases 关键案例

Parkdale Custom Built Furniture v Puxu (1982) 149 CLR 191:

  • Puxu copied Parkdale's furniture designs
  • Conduct likely to mislead consumers into thinking furniture was Parkdale's
  • Principle: Get-up and packaging can be misleading if creates false impression

ACCC v Coles Supermarkets (2014):

  • Coles advertised bread as "Baked Today, Sold Today" when par-baked overseas then finished in store
  • Misleading as consumers believed bread entirely baked fresh in-store
  • Penalty: $2.5 million for misleading representations
Remedies for s18 Breach s18违规的救济
  • Injunctions: Court order to stop conduct
  • Damages: Compensation for loss suffered
  • Corrective advertising: Requirement to correct false statements
  • Penalties: Up to $50 million for corporations, $2.5 million for individuals
  • Refunds/repairs: For consumers who purchased based on misleading conduct

3. Consumer Guarantees (ss 51-59 ACL) 消费者保证

Automatic Rights 自动权利

Consumer guarantees are automatic - apply to all consumer transactions without need for express terms.

Cannot be excluded: Any term purporting to exclude/restrict guarantees is void 任何试图排除/限制保证的条款无效

Guarantees for Goods 商品保证
Guarantee Requirement
s54: Acceptable Quality Fit for all purposes, acceptable appearance/finish, free from defects, safe, durable 适合所有目的、外观可接受、无缺陷、安全、耐用
s55: Fitness for Purpose Fit for particular purpose made known to supplier 适合向供应商说明的特定目的
s56: Match Description Match description/sample/demonstration model 与描述/样品/展示模型相符
s57: Express Warranties Comply with express warranties made 符合做出的明示保证
s58: Spare Parts Manufacturer provides repair facilities and spare parts 制造商提供维修设施和备件
Guarantees for Services 服务保证
  • s60: Due Care and Skill: Services provided with due care and skill 以应有的谨慎和技能提供服务
  • s61: Fitness for Purpose: Fit for specified purpose or achieve specified result 适合指定目的或达到指定结果
  • s62: Reasonable Time: Supplied within reasonable time if not specified 如未指定，在合理时间内提供
Remedies for Breach of Guarantees 违反保证的救济

Major Failure:

  • Goods significantly different from description/sample
  • Goods unfit for purpose and cannot easily be remedied
  • Goods unsafe
  • Consumer can choose: Reject goods and get refund, OR keep goods and claim compensation 拒绝商品并获得退款，或保留商品并索取赔偿

Minor Failure:

  • Supplier gets opportunity to repair/replace (reasonable time)
  • If not fixed: consumer can reject OR keep and claim compensation

4. Unfair Contract Terms (ss 23-28 ACL) 不公平合同条款

When Terms are Unfair 何时条款不公平

A term is unfair if it:

  1. Would cause significant imbalance in parties' rights and obligations 会导致双方权利义务严重失衡
  2. Is not reasonably necessary to protect legitimate interests 不是合理必要保护合法利益
  3. Would cause detriment to consumer if relied on 如果依赖会对消费者造成损害
Application 应用

Unfair contract terms provisions apply to:

  • Standard form contracts (take-it-or-leave-it) 标准格式合同
  • Consumer contracts and small business contracts (under thresholds)
  • Terms that are not main subject matter or price (upfront price and main subject matter excluded)

Effect: Unfair term is void (rest of contract remains) 不公平条款无效(合同其余部分保留)

Examples of Potentially Unfair Terms 可能不公平条款的例子
  • Permitting unilateral variation by supplier
  • Penalizing consumer for breach but not supplier
  • Allowing supplier to terminate at will but not consumer
  • Limiting supplier's vicarious liability
  • Requiring consumer to pay excessive cancellation fees
  • Automatic renewal without adequate notice

5. ACCC Role and Enforcement ACCC的作用与执法

Australian Competition and Consumer Commission (ACCC)

Primary regulator of ACL at Commonwealth level

  • Investigates breaches of ACL
  • Enforces compliance through court proceedings
  • Accepts court enforceable undertakings
  • Issues infringement notices
  • Educates businesses and consumers
  • Recalls unsafe products
Penalties and Sanctions 处罚和制裁

Courts can impose:

  • Pecuniary penalties: Up to $50 million for corporations (or 3x benefit gained, or 30% of turnover) 对公司最高5000万澳元
  • Individual penalties: Up to $2.5 million for individuals 对个人最高250万澳元
  • Injunctions: Orders to stop or prevent conduct
  • Corrective orders: Advertising, refunds, training
  • Disqualification: From managing corporations
  • Compensation: To affected consumers
Recent Enforcement Examples 最近的执法案例

ACCC v Volkswagen (2019): $125 million penalty for diesel emissions scandal

ACCC v Google (2022): $60 million penalty for misleading location data practices

ACCC v Optus (2024): Misleading customers about NBN rollout

6. Product Safety and Recalls 产品安全与召回

Supplier Obligations 供应商义务
  • Must not supply: Goods that don't comply with safety standards 不得供应不符合安全标准的商品
  • Must not supply: Goods subject to permanent ban or recall 不得供应被永久禁止或召回的商品
  • Mandatory reporting: Of deaths, serious injuries, or illnesses 强制报告死亡、重伤或疾病
  • Recall obligations: When Minister orders recall or supplier voluntarily recalls 当部长下令召回或供应商自愿召回时的召回义务
Recall Process 召回流程
  1. Product identified as unsafe or defective
  2. Supplier notifies ACCC of recall
  3. Public notice issued (media, website)
  4. Consumers offered remedy (refund, repair, replacement)
  5. ACCC monitors compliance with recall

Summary 总结

The Australian Consumer Law provides comprehensive protection for consumers and clear obligations for businesses. Key takeaways:

  • s18 prohibits all misleading/deceptive conduct in trade or commerce
  • Consumer guarantees are automatic and cannot be excluded
  • Unfair contract terms in standard form contracts are void
  • Product safety is mandatory with serious penalties for breaches
  • ACCC enforcement includes significant financial penalties

澳大利亚消费者法为消费者提供全面保护，并为企业规定明确义务。关键要点包括第18条禁止所有贸易或商业中的误导/欺骗行为，消费者保证是自动的且不能被排除，标准格式合同中的不公平条款无效，产品安全是强制性的且违规会受到严重处罚，ACCC执法包括重大经济处罚。
