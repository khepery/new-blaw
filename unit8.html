Unit 8: Consumer Protection 单元8：消费者保护

Welcome to Unit 8 欢迎来到第八单元

This unit examines the Australian Consumer Law (ACL) and how it protects consumers from unfair business practices.

本单元研究澳大利亚消费者法（ACL）以及它如何保护消费者免受不公平商业行为的侵害。

Topics Covered 涵盖主题

1. Australian Consumer Law (ACL) 澳大利亚消费者法

A national law that protects consumers and regulates fair trading. Part of the Competition and Consumer Act 2010.

保护消费者和规范公平交易的国家法律。是《2010年竞争与消费者法》的一部分。

2. Misleading and Deceptive Conduct 误导和欺骗性行为

Section 18 ACL prohibits conduct that is likely to mislead or deceive consumers. Includes false advertising and misrepresentation.

ACL第18条禁止可能误导或欺骗消费者的行为。包括虚假广告和虚假陈述。

3. Consumer Guarantees 消费者保证

Automatic rights for consumers when buying goods or services:

消费者在购买商品或服务时的自动权利：

  • Goods must be of acceptable quality 商品必须具有可接受的质量
  • Fit for purpose 适合目的
  • Match description 符合描述
  • Services provided with due care and skill 服务提供时应尽到应有的谨慎和技能

4. Unfair Contract Terms 不公平合同条款

Certain contract terms may be void if they create significant imbalance in rights and obligations.

如果某些合同条款在权利和义务上造成重大不平衡，则可能无效。

5. Product Safety and Recalls 产品安全与召回

Suppliers must ensure products are safe. The ACCC can order recalls for unsafe products.

供应商必须确保产品安全。ACCC可以下令召回不安全的产品。

6. Consumer Rights and Remedies 消费者权利与救济

Consumers can seek:

消费者可以寻求：

  • Refunds 退款
  • Repairs or replacements 维修或更换
  • Compensation for losses 损失赔偿
  • Cancellation of contracts 取消合同

7. ACCC Role and Functions ACCC的作用与职能

The Australian Competition and Consumer Commission enforces consumer law and promotes fair trading.

澳大利亚竞争与消费者委员会执行消费者法并促进公平交易。

Key Learning Outcomes 主要学习成果

By the end of this unit, you should be able to:

在本单元结束时，您应该能够：

  • Understand the scope and purpose of the ACL 理解ACL的范围和目的
  • Identify misleading and deceptive conduct 识别误导和欺骗性行为
  • Apply consumer guarantees to practical scenarios 将消费者保证应用于实际情况
  • Advise consumers on their rights and remedies 就消费者的权利和救济提供建议
