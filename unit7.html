Unit 7: Negligence Part 2 & Negligent Misstatement 单元7：疏忽法（二）与疏忽误述

Welcome to Unit 7 欢迎来到第七单元

This unit builds upon Unit 6 by exploring advanced topics in negligence law, including professional negligence and negligent misstatement.

本单元在第六单元的基础上，探讨疏忽法的高级主题，包括专业疏忽和疏忽误述。

Topics Covered 涵盖主题

1. Professional Negligence 专业疏忽

When professionals (doctors, lawyers, accountants) fail to meet the standard of care expected in their profession.

当专业人士（医生、律师、会计师）未能达到其专业所期望的注意标准时。

2. Negligent Misstatement 疏忽误述

When a careless statement causes economic loss to someone who relied on it. Key case: Hedley Byrne v Heller (1964).

当疏忽的陈述给依赖该陈述的人造成经济损失时。关键案例：Hedley Byrne v Heller（1964）。

3. Pure Economic Loss 纯经济损失

Financial loss not resulting from physical injury or property damage. Generally more difficult to recover.

不是由身体伤害或财产损害造成的经济损失。通常更难获得赔偿。

4. Special Relationships and Reliance 特殊关系与信赖

For negligent misstatement, there must be a special relationship and reasonable reliance on the statement.

对于疏忽误述，必须存在特殊关系并合理依赖该陈述。

5. Defenses to Negligence 疏忽的抗辩

  • Contributory Negligence 共同过失: The plaintiff's own carelessness contributed to the harm 原告自身的疏忽导致了损害
  • Voluntary Assumption of Risk 自愿承担风险: The plaintiff knowingly accepted the risk 原告明知并接受了风险
  • Limitation Periods 时效限制: Time limits for bringing legal action 提起法律诉讼的时间限制

Key Learning Outcomes 主要学习成果

By the end of this unit, you should be able to:

在本单元结束时，您应该能够：

  • Distinguish between ordinary and professional negligence 区分普通疏忽和专业疏忽
  • Apply the principles of negligent misstatement 应用疏忽误述的原则
  • Understand when pure economic loss is recoverable 了解何时可以追回纯经济损失
  • Identify and apply defenses to negligence claims 识别并应用疏忽索赔的抗辩理由
