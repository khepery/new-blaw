Unit 9: Sustainability Part 1 单元9：可持续发展（一）

Welcome to Unit 9 欢迎来到第九单元

This unit introduces sustainability concepts in business, focusing on ESG principles and corporate social responsibility.

本单元介绍商业中的可持续发展概念，重点关注ESG原则和企业社会责任。

Topics Covered 涵盖主题

1. Introduction to Sustainability in Business 商业中的可持续发展概论

Sustainability means meeting present needs without compromising future generations' ability to meet their needs.

可持续发展意味着满足当前需求的同时不损害未来世代满足其需求的能力。

2. ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) ESG（环境、社会、治理）

Environmental 环境: Climate change, pollution, resource use, biodiversity 气候变化、污染、资源使用、生物多样性

Social 社会: Human rights, labor practices, community relations, diversity 人权、劳工实践、社区关系、多样性

Governance 治理: Board composition, ethics, transparency, accountability 董事会组成、道德、透明度、问责制

3. Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) 企业社会责任

A business model where companies integrate social and environmental concerns into their operations and stakeholder interactions.

一种商业模式，公司将社会和环境关切融入其运营和利益相关者互动中。

4. Stakeholder Theory 利益相关者理论

Businesses should consider all stakeholders (employees, customers, suppliers, communities), not just shareholders.

企业应考虑所有利益相关者（员工、客户、供应商、社区），而不仅仅是股东。

5. Triple Bottom Line 三重底线

Measuring success beyond profit:

衡量利润之外的成功：

  • People 人: Social impact 社会影响
  • Planet 地球: Environmental impact 环境影响
  • Profit 利润: Economic performance 经济表现

6. Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 可持续发展目标

The United Nations' 17 global goals for sustainable development by 2030, addressing poverty, inequality, climate change, and more.

联合国到2030年实现可持续发展的17个全球目标，涉及贫困、不平等、气候变化等。

7. Business Ethics and Sustainability 商业道德与可持续发展

The moral principles guiding business decisions toward sustainable practices.

指导商业决策走向可持续实践的道德原则。

Key Learning Outcomes 主要学习成果

By the end of this unit, you should be able to:

在本单元结束时，您应该能够：

  • Define sustainability and its importance in business 定义可持续发展及其在商业中的重要性
  • Explain ESG principles and their application 解释ESG原则及其应用
  • Understand corporate social responsibility 理解企业社会责任
  • Apply stakeholder theory to business decisions 将利益相关者理论应用于商业决策
